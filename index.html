About Us

We offer cupcakes in three sizes: Jumbo Gourmet (filled), Regular & Mini. Plus, we make Cakes, Cake Balls/Pops, Brownie Shots and several other sweet treats!

We are open 7 days a week, beginning Sunday at 11 am till 4 pm. Monday through Friday 8 am till 7 pm, and Saturday 10 am to 5 pm.

Stop in today for a treat or give us a call for an order! We take special orders daily and provide delivery options. For pricing inquiries, please give us a call.
